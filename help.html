    TO KNOW HOW THE GAME WORKS

HOW TO START THE GAME

    1. CLICK TO [ PLAY NOW ] BUTTON FOR START THE GAME.
      2. CLICK TO [ ABOUT ] BUTTON TO KNOW ABOUT ME & GAME.
      3. CHECK OUT CONNECT WITH ME SECTION SO YOU CAN CONNECT WITH ME ON SOCIAL MEDIA.

HOW TO CHOOSE YOUR RIDE

    1. CLICK ON THE IMAGE OF THE CAR YOU WANT TO DRIVE.
      2. IF YOU WANT TO GO BACK THEN CLICK ON THE BUTTON GIVEN ON THE TOP LEFT SIDE.

AFTER CHOOSE YOUR VEHICLE

    1. AFTER CHOOSING YOUR VEHICLE, YOU WILL SEE A GO BUTTON.
      2. START THE GAME BY CLICKING ON THE [ GO ] BUTTON.

HOW TO OPERATE THE GAME

    1. CLICK ON THE [ START ] BUTTON TO PLAY THE GAME.
      2. IF YOU WANT TO GO BACK AND SELECT ANOTHER VEHICLE THEN CLICK ON THE BUTTON GIVEN ON THE TOP LEFT SIDE.
      3. IF YOU WANT TO SEE YOUR SCORE, YO CAN LOOK AT THE TOP RIGHT SIDE.

HOW THE GAME FUNCTIONS WORK

    1. YOU CAN SEE THE CAR YOU CHOOSE BELOW.
      2. ALSO YOU CAN SEE THE OPPONENT CAR ON TOP.
      3. ALL YOU HAVE TO DO IS KEEP YOUR CAR MOVING FORWARD BY AVOIDING ALL THOSS CARS.
      4. YOU HAVE GOT TWO BUTTONS TO GO LEFT & RIGHT. YOU CAN USE THOSE BUTTONS TO MOVE YOUR CAR FORWARD.

AFTER YOUR & ENEMY CAR COLLIDE

    1. AFTER YOUR & ENEMY CAR COLLIDE YOU WILL SEE SOME OPTIONS IN FRONT OF YOU.
      2. YOU CAN SEE THE COMPLETE SCORE OF THAT GAME THERE.
      3. YOU CAN PLAY THE GAME AGAIN BY CLICKING ON THE [ PLAY AGAIN ] BUTTON.
